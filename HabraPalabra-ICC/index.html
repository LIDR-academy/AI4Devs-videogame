    How to Play

    Connect adjacent letters to form words.

    Letters can be connected horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

    Words are automatically validated when you release your mouse or finger.

    Longer words give you more points!

    Using Hints

    You start each game with 3 hints. When stuck, click the Hint button to reveal the first letter of a valid word.

    How hints work:

    • The game finds valid words you haven't discovered yet
    • A random word is selected
    • The first letter of that word pulsates with a bright glow for 3 seconds
    • Your hint count decreases by 1

    Use hints strategically when you can't find any more words!
